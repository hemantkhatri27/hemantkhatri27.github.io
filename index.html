Maiden Sample Pack

Maiden is our debut sample pack. Filled with hard-hitting drums, crispy percussion, thick bass one shots and melodic loops, Maiden contains the perfect sounds for your next Future Bass/Pop/Hip-hop/RnB/Trap track.

A total of 50 samples are included within the pack. The pack is absolutely FREE of cost and 100% royalty free. Each sample is BPM & Key labelled as well. Included within the pack are:

  • 10 Kicks
  • 10 Snares
  • 10 Percussion Loops
  • 10 Bass One Shots
  • 10 Melodic Loops
